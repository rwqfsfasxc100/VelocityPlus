[1.2.0]

1="* Added Auto Repair"
1.1="* Performs repairs based on several criteria"
1.2="* Modes: "
1.2.1="* Cost effective - performs the most cost effective operation to. Default mode."
1.2.2="* Only repair - only performs repair operations to meet the desired repair status"
1.2.3="* Only replace - automatically replaces all equipment"
1.2.4="* Maximise profit - performs maintenance to procure the most cash from the derelict. Makes the ship worth the most in it's current form, and will not replace equipment to squeeze extra cash (green numbers when selling will indicate when it's effective to do so)"
1.3="* Operators: "
1.3.1="* Minimum money - The absolute minimum available cash available to perform auto repairs. Insurance is included within the calculation. Defaults to 100,000"
1.3.2="* Minimum insurance - The minimum available insurance needed to perform auto repairs. Having this set to zero will allow for money from your bank account to repair. Defaults to 0"
1.3.3="* Maximum repair cost - The most that auto repair can spend in a single repair action. Defaults to 250,000"
1.3.4="* Maximum replace cost - The most that auto repair can spend to replace equipment. Defaults to 2,500,000"
1.3.5="* Target repair percentage - The repair percentage that auto repair will aim to achieve based on matching criteria. May go over this amount if the last repair performed crosses the threshold. Cost effective priority mode will replace equipment if it is more cost effective than achieving this target. Ignored by only replace and max profit modes. Defaults to 70%"
1.4="* For safety reasons, Auto Repair is performed only when opening the Repairs menu at Enceladus Prime."
1.5="* Auto Repair is disabled by default (I know people don't read these so I would rather not get complaints by this being active upon updating)"
2="* Minding Virtual Flight Service shader changes are now compatible with versions including the addition of the premium subscription."
3="* HevLib checker script now works properly and can read MV2.2"
